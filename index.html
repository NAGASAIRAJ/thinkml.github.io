We inspire people to fall in love with Machine Learning.

thinkML organize Machine Learning and Deep Learning workshops, create open sourced online materials and curate amazing first experiences with technology.

What is ThinkML?

ThinkML is a non-profit organisation that helps promote, improve, develop, democratise and decentralise Machine Learning.It's an organisation of researchers, developers, enthusiasts and free-thinkers who believe in the concept of free and safe implementation of Machine Learning. A true open source community that shares, collaborates and innovates.

ThinkML is also a hub for various college chapters that promote and democratize Machine Learning concepts, provide resources that turn amateurs into adept users of Machine Learning and sponsor students to publish papers.

Democratize

ThinkML believes in making Machine Learning accessible to anyone and everyone willing to invest time and effort to learn. This is achieved with a system that was created by the people, which is for the people and consists of the people.

Develop

We ensure to contribute towards the development of the ever-growing field of Machine Learning in a controlled and coherent manner.

Decentralize

Machine Learning was created to be shared and explored by everyone willing to. At ThinkML we believe in just that and strive to give access to the whole community with every resource available to us
